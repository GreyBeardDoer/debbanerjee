100 Days Of Coding: Day 1 Expanding Cards Project

There are 2 different approaches to learn -

  1. Learning many skills just enough to get working knowledge about many different skill sets.
  2. Learning just one skill deeply and be an expert in it.

You can read about why I transitioned from the first type of learning to the second type here.

At the beginning of January 2023, I decided that I shall be learning just ONE thing consistently for 6 months and see how deep I can go about it.

After deciding that, I had to pick what I wanted to learn deeply for the first 6 months of 2023.

I have many interests but the 2 things that can keep me awake at night are - writing and coding. I am an amateur in both these skills. That's why I decided that I will devote the whole of 2023 to master these skills.

The first 6 months from January to June' 2023 I'll learn coding. And the later half of the year I'll master the art of digital writing.

So, here it is!
